The Values we stand for

MacAppStudio is built on a strong foundation of culture which is fundamental to our client success and essential in how we work together.

The customer first approach

We only win when our customers succeed. We work tirelessly to solve their problem and maximize their efficiency.

Act with ownership

We are accountable for the company’s and the customer’s growth. Being responsible at work is crucial.

Doing best in what we do

May it be any work, focus on the work and create an impact. Be the best in your field.

No resume policy

A printed paper can’t tell us the potential of a person. We see people as humans with emotions.

Value idea over hierarchy

We welcome great ideas which has a great potential, from anyone regarless their roles and hierarchy.

Respect for individual

Treat everyone equal from the CEO to the house keeping and respect their differences and capacity.

Room for experimentation

We grow by experimenting new things and failing fast. Failure makes us smarter and we get back stronger.

Being honest to oneself

Honesty is the mother of all virtues. We respect and encourage people who are honest in their work.

Pushing the limit

We encourage and motivate people to take risks and set the bar high. This is how we can grow together.

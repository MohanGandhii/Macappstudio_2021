Overview

Started in 2012, TALE was set up with the objective to provide affordable quality language education to children from all walks of life and across geographical boundaries. They are part of an Educational Revolution that seeks to infuse technology in learning and thereby, provide the latest and best pedagogy in language skills development.

Problem Faced

TALE wanted to create a comprehensive language learning platform that aims to give children the English skills they need to be competent global citizens. The platform should seamlessly be able to deliver the course content that TALE has created to the intended student and levels.

Goal

To create an awesome end to end cloud based learning platform for English which is adaptive, interactive and progressive to the 1,50,000 students of TALE institutes.

Solution

MacAppStudio designed and developed the end to end platform for delivering the English language training to all the institutions subscribed to TALE. The platform has several modules including content creation dashboard (to create end to end content), CRM dashboard (to onboard schools), school dashboard (to register students and see course progress) and student module (to take courses). The platform is able to deliver over 10,00,000 hours of training to the students in realtime with minimum internet bandwidth requirements in case of remotely location schools. The course dashboard for students has an attractive design with kids friendly UI/UX flow and also gamification to enhance the kids engagement.

Technology

Current Status

The platform was launched in July 2018 in CSI Ewart School in Chennai and within one year it has been adopted by over 100+ institutes all over India. Currently TALE delivers end to end English learning content for various class students with over 10,00,000 hours of training seamlessly via the platform. With over 1,50,000+ students accessing the platform on a regular basis TALE has become the #1 English learning solution in India.
